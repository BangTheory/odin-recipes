The Glummer Potion

Misery in a bottle. Removes all hope and happiness with one sip!

Caution: Can lead to permanent madness

Ingredients

  • Eye of Newt
  • Worm Tongue
  • Rats Tail
  • Blue Food Coloring
  • Red Food Coloring
  • Optional
    • Sazon
    • Lemon Juice

Steps

  1. Boil water in a large cauldron
  2. Crush eye of newt and add to cauldron
  3. Slowly stir in worm tongue until cauldron begins to glow
  4. Add rats tail and food coloring and optional ingredients
  5. Remove Cauldron from heat and allow potion to cool before serving(makes 2 servings)

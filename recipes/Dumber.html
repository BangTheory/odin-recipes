The Dumber Potion

Kills brain cells. Cannot be regenerated

Caution: Can lead to death

Ingredients

  • Eye of Newt
  • Monster Energy Drink
  • Vodka
  • Blue Food Coloring
  • Yellow Food Coloring

Steps

  1. Crush eye of newt and refrigerate over night
  2. Mix eye of newt powder with Monster energy drink
  3. Boil water in a large cauldron
  4. Drink Vodka
  5. Add Monster energy drink/eye of newt to cauldron
  6. Add food colorings
  7. Remove cauldron from heat and allow potion to cool before serving(makes 1 serving)

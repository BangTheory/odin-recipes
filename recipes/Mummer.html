The Mummer Potion

Temporarily disables speech.

Caution: May be permanent

Ingredients

  • Eye of Newt
  • Scrugm
  • Flax Seed Oil
  • Flaming Hot Cheetos
  • Opium
  • Blue Food Coloring
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Boil water in a medium cauldron
  2. Remove the mooga from the scrugm and combine with eye of newt
  3. Add mixture to cauldron
  4. Coat Cheetos in flax seed oil and slowly add to cauldron
  5. Add Opium to Cauldron
  6. Remove cauldron from heat and add food colorings
  7. Add sugar to taste
    • Caution: Do not taste more than once
  8. Cool before serving(makes 1 serving)
